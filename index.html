SUMMER

Summer is the hottest of the four temperate seasons, occurring after spring and before autumn.

WINTER

Winter is the coldest season of the year in polar and temperate climates. It occurs after autumn and before spring

RAINY

It starts from the month of June and lasts till September. Rainy season brings relief from the heat.

SPRING

Spring, also known as springtime, is one of the four temperate seasons, succeeding winter and preceding summer.
